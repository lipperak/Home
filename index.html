Hi !
I am a second year PhD student in flow mechanics at LadHyX. (Laboratoire d'Hydrodynamique de l'Ecole Polytechnique) under the supervison of Sebastien Michelin and Michael Benzaquen
My research focuses mainly on hydrodynamics at small length scales (micron-size) and especially on the behavior of single and collective self-propelled particles. Thoses systems are good candidates as biomimetic alternatives to biological microorganisms in addition to potential device for controlled applications in the field of biomedical engineering (drug-delivery).
Before I had plunged into the low-Reynolds fluid dynamics field of research I graduated from the Mechanical Engineering Department of Ecole des Ponts et Chaussées (GMM) and from Advanced Aeronautical Engineering Master of Sciences at Imperial College of London (AAE) .
Finally, I discovered the fascinating and thrilling world of research thanks to Thierry Ondarçuhu through a project on wetting phenomenon at the nano-scale.
